The tablet market isn't in great shape, but Apple is still convinced that the iPad represents the future of mobile computing.
That's where the Pro models come in: They're designed to bring serious horsepower to everyday tasks in hopes that people could use them to
replace traditional computers. Now we've got a new one,
the 10.5-inch iPad Pro, which replaces the 9.7-inch model we reviewed last year. After a bit of hands-on time, one thing is clear:
If you're looking for a premium tablet, this is one slate you can't ignore.
